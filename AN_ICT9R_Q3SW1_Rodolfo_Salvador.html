BREAKING BAD

Breaking Bad to many people is one of the best drama/thriller tv series aired as it has won about 16 emmys and many more awards during its 5 season premiere the show talks about a chemistry teacher, Walter White, who finds out he has cancer and doesnt his family to suffer from paying his medicine and treatments so he asks a previous student of his, Jesse Pinkman, to make methamphetamine and sell it along the way his brother in law, Hank Shrader, tries to catch him.

Characters

Walter White

Played by Bryan Cranston, Walter White is the main protagonist of the series. He starts out as a chemistry teacher and after being diagnosed with cancer he partners with Jesse to make and sell methamphetamine

Jesse Pinkman

Played by Aaron Paul, Jesse Pinkman is Walter White's former student and partner in the methamphetamine selling. Before partnering with Walter he was already selling other types of drugs

Skyler White

Played by Anna Gunn, Skyler White is Walter White's wife. At first she doesnt know abut the business of Walter but slowly learns more about it, She is also the most annoying character in the series.
