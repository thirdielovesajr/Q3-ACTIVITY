About Fire Prevention Month

Fire Prevention Month is observed every March in the Philippines to raise awareness about the dangers of fire and promote fire safety measures. The month-long event is spearheaded by the Bureau of Fire Protection, and it involves various activities such as fire drills, symposia, and public information campaigns.

Fire Prevention Tips

Here are some tips on how to prevent fire accidents:

Tip Description
1 Keep flammable materials away from heat sources.
2 Check electrical wiring and appliances regularly.
3 Do not smoke cigarattes indoors.
4 Keep a fire extinguisher at home and know how to use it.
5 Make an escape plan and practice it with your family.

For more information, please visit the Bureau of Fire Protection website.
